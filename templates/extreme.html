Extreme Risk
Triggers:
  • NWS issued Excessive Heat Warning or are likely to be in an Excessive Heat Warning within the next 0.5-2 days
  • Extreme Heat weather anticipated to continue for multiple days
    • Heat Index Range: 125-140
    Health Implications: Heatstroke/sunstroke is highly likely with continued exposure. This is a multi-day heat event

  • Athletes should drink 2+ cups of water and/or sports drinks in the hour before competition/practice. Sports drinks should contain no more than 6-8% of carbohydrates and electrolytes to enhance fluid consumption.
